UNCS2D (UltiNaruto Counter-Strike 2D)

So to begin with my first dev wiki I coded a remake of Counter-Strike for old gen PCs or at least weak PCs such as netbooks or cheap notebooks.

The weapons are :
– M4A1
– Deagle
– MP5
– Anaconda
– XM8
– AK-47
– Kriss Super V
– Knife
– M60
– P90

– 30/09/2014 (v4):

– Now maps are loaded from files externally

– Added map editor

– 14/03/2012 (v3):

– Collision system fully reworked
– Weapons have been modified
– Render engine fully reworked
– Added a map
– Keys :
– Player 1 :
Z,Q,S,D \\ Move
Spacebar \\ Shoot
A \\ Previous Weapon
E \\ Next Weapon
– Player 2 :
Directional Arrows \\ Move
Right Control \\ Shoot
L \\ Previous Weapon
M \\ Next Weapon

– "Suicide" action deleted
– Weapon system has changed
– New weapons available

– Damages are no more random

Spacebar \\ Shoot
1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 \\ Select Weapons

Directional Arrows \\ Move
Numeric Pad 0 \\ Shoot
